Chapati

Description

Chapati is a soft flatbread widely eaten in Kenya. It is made from wheat flour and cooked on a hot pan. It is commonly served with beef stew, vegetables or tea.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups wheat flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl
  2. Add water gradually and knead into smooth dough
  3. Add oil and knead for 5 more minutes
  4. Divide dough into small balls and roll flat
  5. Cook on a hot pan until golden brown on both sides
  6. Serve hot

Githeri

Description

Githeri is a traditional Kenyan dish made from boiled maize and beans mixed together. It is nutritious, affordable and widely eaten across Kenya.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize
  • 1 cup beans
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 tomatoes chopped
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak maize and beans overnight
  2. Boil together until soft
  3. Fry onions and tomatoes in oil
  4. Add the boiled maize and beans
  5. Mix well and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Serve hot

ugali

description

Ugali is a staple food in Kenya made from maize flour. It is simple, filling and pairs well with vegetables, meat or fish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 3 cups water
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in a sufuria
  2. Add maize flour gradually while stirring
  3. Keep stirring until firm
  4. Serve hot
